WHO ARE WE

We are a dedicated group of support workers on a mission to help elderly individuals and people with disabilities achieve digital independence.

What we stand for?

At ElderlyTechSupport, we share the Ciecro view on aging. We believe that living right means the early part of life is the inferior part. Our support workers unwavering commitment to support, self-improvement, and daily learning drives our mission to empower those who dare to change the world."

Why do we have to do this?

Our team have witnessed many elderly people and individuals with disabilities struggle with computer literacy, often falling victim to scams. Traditional institutions may not cater to their unique learning capacities. Our passionate support workers aim to bridge this gap by providing personalized assistance, empowering them to navigate the digital world independently and securely.

What do we do?

We do this to provide person-centered care focused on technology education and assistance, ensuring that elderly individuals and those with disabilities can comfortably and confidently use digital tools, enhancing their quality of life.
